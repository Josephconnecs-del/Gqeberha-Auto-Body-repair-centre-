Over 40 Years of Excellence

Founded in 1983 by Dolf Jonker, Gqeberha Auto Body (formerly Autotrust) has grown into one of the best-equipped facilities in South Africa. We specialize in complex repairs for prestige and luxury vehicles, ensuring a seamless collision repair experience.

Our Specialized Services

Major Structural Repairs

Certified to handle heavy structural damage for all vehicle types utilizing state-of-the-art chassis straightening.

Aluminium Repair Facility

Operating South Africa’s only stand-alone facility strictly dedicated to aluminium-bodied vehicles.

Speed Shop

Specialized drive-thru quotation and rapid repair systems for minor dents, scratches, and damages.
